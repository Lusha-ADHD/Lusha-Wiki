This section gathers narrative information about Lusha: world, characters, places, objects, quests, and vocabulary. It should stay clear for new players while leaving room for more detailed pages later.

## What Belongs Here

- An overview of Lusha's world.
- Character pages, including each character's role in the game experience.
- Places and regions, once their official names are confirmed.
- Gameplay concepts such as quests, rewards, and progression.
- Consistent vocabulary that keeps real-life tasks distinct from in-game objectives.

## Writing Rules

Universe pages should distinguish game story from real family routines. A chore is an everyday action; a quest is an in-game objective. This distinction helps families understand the connection between playful motivation and real life.
