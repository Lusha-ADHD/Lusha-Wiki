The **Settings** section gathers options that help adapt Lusha to the child and family. It gives access to game settings, as well as app administration areas.

  <img src="/assets/how-to-use-lusha/app-settings/welcome.png" alt="Lusha Settings screen with Subscription, Parent Settings, Game settings, Account, Feedback, and About." />
  <figcaption>From the Settings tab, open Game settings to adjust the game experience.</figcaption>
</figure>

To personalize the game experience, go to **Settings**, then open **Game settings**. Lusha lets you adjust the game experience in detail so it can better fit younger children, children with dyspraxia, or simply each child's comfort while playing.

These settings can be useful if the child gets tired quickly, if controls are hard to handle, if the phone is older, or if the sound environment needs to stay calm.

### Sound

You can adjust the game volume at three levels: master volume, sound effects volume, and background music volume. You can also turn off the child's audio responses if they are distracting, overstimulating, or not adapted to the context of use.

<figure class="lusha-screenshot">
  <img src="/assets/how-to-use-lusha/app-settings/sound.png" alt="Game Settings screen with Master Volume, Sound Effects Volume, Music Volume, and Child audio response." />
  <figcaption>Audio settings let you adjust master volume, sound effects, music, and voice responses separately.</figcaption>
</figure>

### Joysticks

You can adjust the joystick position to fit the child's needs, height, dominant hand, or the way they hold the phone. Movement sensitivity can also be changed to make navigation smoother or more precise.

<figure class="lusha-screenshot">
  <img src="/assets/how-to-use-lusha/app-settings/joystick.png" alt="Game Settings screen showing Joystick movements and Sensitivity to Movements." />
  <figcaption>The joystick can be adjusted to make movement more comfortable.</figcaption>
</figure>

### Character Appearance

You can modify the character chosen initially and adjust their appearance: skin tone, clothes, and hair. If you want to change the character's gender, contact us at [contact@lusha.care](mailto:contact@lusha.care).

<figure class="lusha-screenshot">
  <img src="/assets/how-to-use-lusha/app-settings/character.png" alt="Game Settings screen with the Edit button for changing character appearance." />
  <figcaption>The Edit button reopens character customization.</figcaption>
</figure>

### Graphics

You can adjust the game's graphic quality. This setting helps adapt Lusha to older or less powerful phones, so the experience stays smoother.

<figure class="lusha-screenshot">
  <img src="/assets/how-to-use-lusha/app-settings/graphism.png" alt="Game Settings screen with the Graphism setting between Low and High." />
  <figcaption>The Graphism setting helps balance visual quality and performance depending on the phone.</figcaption>
</figure>

### Language

You can change the game language if needed. Lusha is available in English, French, Spanish, Italian, and Brazilian Portuguese.
