# Lusha Wiki Glossary

This glossary defines preferred terminology for the Lusha Wiki. Use it to keep wording consistent across pages, translations, SEO metadata, and AI-assisted edits.

The glossary is intentionally incomplete. Add terms as product wording becomes official. French is the editorial source language, but English is currently served from the public root path.

## Naming Rules

- `Lusha` is the product name and should not be translated.
- `Dygie` is the company name and should not be translated.
- Prefer clear public-facing terms over internal development names.
- Keep gameplay terms distinct from real-life routine terms.
- When a term is uncertain, mark it as `TBD` instead of inventing a final label.

## Terms To Confirm

These concepts need official wording before broad use:

- Name of the camp or central game location.
- Names of recurring characters or guides.
- Names of reward currencies, badges, objects, or collectibles.
- Official names for parent dashboard features.
- Official wording for app modules, programs, or activities.
- Final product descriptor: `game`, `app`, `support game`, `ADHD support app`, or another approved phrase.

## Translation Notes

- Add columns for German, Italian, Spanish, and Brazilian Portuguese when translations are confirmed.
- Do not translate character, place, or feature names unless the product intentionally localizes them.
